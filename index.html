Welcome to my RPG!

Below the commands you need to know if you want to play:

  • Normal Attack: turn.playerAttacks(victimName);
  • Special Attack: turn.playerAttacks(victimName, true)
  • Game Stats: game.watchStats();

For example: victimName = Carl

Enjoy!


Rules:

5 warriors. Each one of them plays once per trun. No more hp and you're dead.

You win if you are the last one alive or if you are still alive after 10 turns.


Roles:

Assassin

  • Hp: 6
  • Dmg: 6
  • Mana: 20
  • Special Attack: Do not deal and do not receive damage for the current turn. The attacked victim will receive 7 dmg on then next turn. If the victim is still alive the assasin takes 7 dmg. Cost: 20 mana.

Berzerker

  • Hp: 8
  • Dmg: 4
  • Mana: 0
  • Special Attack: Give +1 dmg for the game but cost 1 hp. Cost: 0 mana, 1 hp.

Fighter

  • Hp: 12
  • Dmg: 4
  • Mana: 40
  • Special Attack: Deal 5 dmg and give himself 2 points shield for the current turn. Cost: 20 mana.

Monk

  • Hp: 8
  • Dmg: 2
  • Mana: 100
  • Special Attack: Deal 8 hp. Cost: 25 mana.

Paladin

  • Hp: 16
  • Dmg: 3
  • Mana: 160
  • Special Attack: Deal 4 dmg and heal 5 hp. Cost: 40 mana.

Pirate

  • Hp: 12
  • Dmg: 3
  • Mana: 100
  • Special Attack: Deal 5 dmg. Cost: 20 mana.

Wizard

  • Hp: 10
  • Dmg: 2
  • Mana: 200
  • Special Attack: Deal 7 dmg. Cost: 25 mana.
